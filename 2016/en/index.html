January 23, 2015 at Lohmann & Birkner GmbH in Berlin

Keynote: Anil Madhavapeddy

"What happens when we use what's best for a change?"

Object-oriented development and its platforms have been well-established for years in software development. By now, a new arsenal of techniques, technologies, platforms, and languages are determining the development practices of the future. These are often superior to traditional OO techniques, languages and tools. Some examples:

  • functional programming
  • reactive programming,
  • microservice architectures,
  • persistent data structures and databases

BOB is the forum for developers, architects and builders to explore technologies beyond the mainstream and to discover the best tools available today for building software. Our goal is for all participants to leave the conference with new ideas to improve development back at the ranch.

We cooperate with :clojureD on the next day - January 24 - also in Berlin.
