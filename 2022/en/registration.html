Registration

BOB 2022 takes place on 11th March 2022 as a virtual conference (online). Participation in the conference requires a registration. The registration fee of BOB 2022 depends on when you register. We also offer a student discount. Further, we would like to support participants from groups under-represented in technology.

Student regular Grants
Online, until FIXME (early bird) EUR 5 EUR 10 see below
Online, until 11th March 2022 EUR 15 EUR 30 see below
Register

Notes

BOB 2022 also offers several tutorials, which are included in the conference fee. However, limited capacities require that you register for each tutorial individually.

Discount eligibility

If you are eligible for our student discount, you must register for the conference using your university or college issued e-mail address.

Grants

The BOB conference supports participants from groups under-represented in technology (e.g. women). Please send us an e-mail before you register if you require financial support.
