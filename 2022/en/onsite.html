Onsite or Online

Dear folks interested in BOB!

We want to hold BOB in-person: We did have a great virtual BOB 2021 - but we'd love to see you in brickspace, and dial BOB-related social interactions up to 11!

This will only be possible if we can establish a high level of safety regarding COVID. We're aiming for a level comparable to "regular" public life, for example using public transport. We're aware it's not certain we can achieve that level given the Omicron wave, about which there's a lot we don't know yet. (But be assured: We're closely and continually monitoring the situation.)

At this point in time, we can't rule out that we'll be able to hold BOB in-person. That's why we're leaving that option open, while also considering the option of a virtual BOB.

One thing is certain though: BOB 2022 will happen on March 11!

If BOB is to happen in-person, we will need to enact measures to reach an acceptable level of safety. We can't say with certainty which measures exactly we'll enact, but they might include:

  • entry only with proof of vaccination; proof of a "booster" shot may be required
  • checkin via the Corona-Warn-App
  • lateral-flow test on-site before entry (or proof of test from the day of the BOB)
  • masks required at all times indoors
  • frequent and thorough airing of indoor spaces
  • breaks and lunch outside (we might organize a roof)

We might also have to follow additional regulations, depending on Berlin legislation at the time of the event. However, we will evaluate safety measures independently of the then-current regulations and consider enacting measures that are not required.

We look forward to your feedback - what conditions we should create for you to safely enjoy BOB, and which could be particularly effective. We've created a survey for that purpose, where you can also leave your e-mail address if you want to be informed of developments regarding BOB 2022.

We will make a final decision if BOB happens in-person or virtually until

January 17, 2022

We'll post on our homepage and, of course, inform on Twitter. We'll also start ticket sales on January 17.
