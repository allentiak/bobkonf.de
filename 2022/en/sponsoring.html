Sponsorship for BOB conference 2022

BOB is the forum for developers, architects and builders to explore and discover the best tools available today for building software. Our goal is for all participants to leave the conference with new ideas to improve development back at the ranch.

  • Professional: Top keynote speakers, two tracks of talks and a tutorial track
  • Invigorating: ~160 developers, architects, makers come to BOB every year
  • Diverse: Participants from groups underrepresented in tech receive extensive support

Bronze status

Bronze (€333) includes

  • logo and mention on the BOB web page
  • logo on the program leaflet PDF, poster at the virtual conference site

Silver status

Silver (€500) includes everything from bronze plus:

  • brief mention in the opening talk

Gold status

Gold (€1000) includes everything from silver plus:

  • explicit mention in the opening talk
  • own booth at conference
  • prominent display on BOB web page

Platinum status

Platinum (€1500) includes everything from gold plus:

  • a presentation slot in one of the breaks (*)
  • most prominent display on BOB web page

(**) Please be prepared to record a video with your talk in case the conference goes digital.
(***) In the event of an analogue conference only.
