Evening program

Thursday, 23.2.2017

We have reserved seats in the I Due Forni, start is 7pm. Just come by, eat and drink something and discuss with other conference participants. This evening is "pay for yourself". You can reach the restaurant with underground U2, stop is either Senefelderplatz or Rosa-Luxemburg-Platz.

Friday, 24.2.2017

To celebrate BOB 2017, we have organized a dinner directly after the conference. The dinner event takes place at Cafe Morgenrot. You can reach the cafe from the conference venue in approx 30 minutes (U8 from Paracelsus-Bad direction Hermannstraße to Rosenthaler Platz; from there two stops with Tram M1 direction Niederschönhausen to Schwedter Straße). The dinner starts between 6:30pm and 7pm, directly after the conference.

The dinner event is not included in the conference fee, it costs 20 Euro extra. Included is vegetarian/vegan food and drinks for about 10 Euro. Please make sure that you pay drinks for yourself once you get over the 10 Euro limit.

Important: You need to register for the conference dinner.
