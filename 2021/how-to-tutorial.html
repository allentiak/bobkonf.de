How tutorials work at BOB

There are two parallel tutorial tracks during BOB in two adjacent rooms on the upper left hand side of the Gather space. The tutorials will be accompanied by a BOB staff member to help with technical problems and admission control.

Tutorials #1

Tutorials #1 is the larger of the two rooms for tutorials that allow 50 participants. In this room, there are spotlighted seats for the speakers on the left hand side marked by orange circles below the chairs. The persons on the spotlighted seats can be seen and heard by all participants in the room and screens that are shared from the spotlighted positions are broadcast to everybody in the room.

There also is a shared whiteboard in the room that can be accessed by pressing x.

You can use a combination of screen sharing, chat, and the shared whiteboard to transport your content.

Tutorials #2

Tutorials #2 is the smaller room for tutorials that allow 20 or less participants. The top part of this room is a private space, which means that everybody in the upper space can be seen and heard by everybody else in that area. This allows for a more interactive experience in the smaller tutorial setting.

There also is a shared whiteboard in the room that can be accessed by pressing x.

Additionally, there are private work areas in the lower half to be able to split out groups during the tutorial.

You can use a combination of screen sharing, chat, the shared whiteboard, and group sessions to transport your content.
