This year's conference is a little different!

How do I join the conference?

  • Tickets: get yours here.
  • Venue: we will meet in a space hosted on gather.town. Feel free to check out their walkthrough and familiarise yourself with the site.
    On the day of the conference, allow ~10 minutes for login, setting up your avatar, etc.

    The exact URL will be sent to the e-mail address you used to register for BOBkonf 2021 at some point during the 24 hours before the conference starts, and also appear on this page on 2021-02-26 08:00 UTC+1. You will need to sign in with the e-mail address provided when buying your ticket.

    gather.town works best in Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox.

Where did Gather get my name?!

We are using the names provided on ticket purchase for the names in gather. If you'd prefer a different display name, please edit your ticket accordingly by 2021-02-24.

HOW do I navigate the conference space?

You will need, ideally, a headset and webcam, and a computer with a stable internet connection (gather.town is mobile accessible, but we are optimising for computer use). If you'd like to explore a little beforehand, here's gather's own live walkthrough.

There are too many things happening and people talking on the screen and I'm overwhelmed.

Gather has a "quiet mode" that reduces interaction distance to one square – so only people standing directly next to you in all directions will be able to hear you. Click on your name (in the middle on the bottom of the page) and select "quiet mode", or press CTRL/CMD+U for the same result. We especially recommend this setting during talks (you will still be able to hear the video).
If that won't cut it, you can log out and back in at any time.

I'm still having trouble – where do I get help?

Contact us via e-mail at help2021@bobkonf.de, or on twitter @bobkonf. Within gather, you can private message anyone with [staff] in their name or come to the help desk for support.
