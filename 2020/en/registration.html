Registration

BOB 2020 takes place 28th February 2020 in Berlin. Participation in the conference requires a registration. The registration fee of BOB 2020 depends on when you register. We also offer a student discount and a discount for participants of :clojured. Further, we would like to support participants from groups under-represented in technology.

Student / :clojured regular / :clojured Grants
Online, until 20th January 2020 (early bird) 75/65 EUR 150/130 EUR see below
Online, until 27th February 2020 90/80 EUR 180/160 EUR see below
on-site, 28th February 2020 120 EUR 240 EUR -
Register

Important

Access to the conference venue requires signing a Non-Disclosure Agreement, WIFI usage requires signing another agreement. You have to sign these agreements at the day of the conference. Please check now whether you will sign the agreements: Non-Disclosure Agreement, WIFI-Agreement. Please contact us via email at konferenz@bobkonf.de for questions regarding these agreements.

Notes

The conference fee includes snacks, drinks and lunch.

BOB 2020 also offers several tutorials, which are included in the conference fee. However, limited capacities require that you register for each tutorial individually.

Discount eligibility

If you are eligible for our student discount, you must provide a valid, current student ID at the day of the conference. If you fail to provide a student ID, you have to pay the full registration fee.

To be eligible for the :clojured discount, you have to present a ticket for :clojured when entering BOB conference.

Grants

The BOB conference supports participants from groups under-represented in technology (e.g. women). The following options are available:
  1. 30% discount on the conference fee for participants from groups under-represented in technology. If you are eligible, please write an e-mail before you register. You will then receive a discount code.
  2. 10% discount on the conference fee if you recruit other participants from groups under-represented in technology. Please write an e-mail before you register. You will then receive a discount code.
  3. We have a very limited number of free tickets for the conference. Please write us an e-mail before you register and tell us why you need this kind of support.

Child care

BOB offers child care for the entire day at no additional cost. Child care will be provided in the building of the conference. If you would like to sign up for this, please contact us via e-mail.
