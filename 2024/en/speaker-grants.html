Speaker Grants

BOB is pleased to announce the availability of Speaker Grants to support speakers from groups under-represented in technology. We specifically seek women speakers and speakers who would not be able to attend the conference for financial reasons. The goal is to make BOB a more diverse, useful and enjoyable event.

Eligibility

Speaker Grants are available for all speakers accepted at BOB 2024 from under-represented groups in technology.

Selection

Applications will be reviewed, and will be accepted based upon need and impact. All information will be kept confidential by the committee.

 

Grants

We currently have a limited number of Speaker Grants available that will cover reasonable travel costs, travel assistance and accommodation for the nights before and after BOB – i.e. from 14 March, 2024 to 16 March, 2024. These grants are capped at â‚¬600 per person.

Application

Please include your reasons for seeking a grant directly with your talk proposal—there's a dedicated text box for this purpose toward the end of the application process.

All conference attendees are expected to respect the BOB policies.

If you have any questions, please contact diversity at bobkonf dot de.

Notifications: Recipients will be notified along with their talk acceptance by 5 December, 2023.
